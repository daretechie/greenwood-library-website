About Greenwood Library

Our Mission

Founded in 1995, Greenwood Library strives to foster a love for reading and learning in our community. We offer free access to books, workshops, and cultural events for all ages.

Our Team

Our dedicated staff includes librarians, educators, and volunteers committed to making knowledge accessible to everyone.
